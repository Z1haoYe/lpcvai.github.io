Track 3

Monocular Depth Estimation

The participants will estimate the depth of every single pixel on given RGB images (monocular dense depth estimation). The images are captured indoor and outdoor under varying lighting conditions. Compared to other monocular depth estimation challenges, this track focuses on evaluating the efficiency of a model on mobile devices and its robustness under varying lighting conditions. Each team will be given 1,000 indoor and 1,000 outdoor images. Both indoor and outdoor images may have different lighting conditions, e.g., a parking lot image captured at daytime, and a living room captured at dinner time with light off, etc. The accuracy will be calculated using AbsRel, RMSE, RMSE log, 1, 2, 3, etc. The final score will be calculated by dividing one of the accuracy scores (TBD) by the execution time.
