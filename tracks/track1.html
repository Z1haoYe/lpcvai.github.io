Track 1

Image classification for different lighting conditions and styles

Information

  • Sponsor: Qualcomm Technology Inc.
  • Competition: 2025 Low-Power Computer Vision Challenge (LPCVC 2025)
  • Vision Task: Image classification on images captured under various lighting conditions and formats.
  • Hardware: The submitted model will be evaluated under the following platforms on Qualcomm AI Hub:
    • Samsung Galaxy
    • TBD
  • Software: Qualcomm Neural Network (QNN), Qualcomm AI Hub
  • Reimbursement policy: TBD
  • Technical Support: Subscribe to the newsletter or join the Slack workspace.
  • Evaluation: TBD
  • Prizes: TBD

Prerequisites

Please register an account on Qualcomm AI Hub and specify “LPCVC 2025” during registration.
Visit qai-hub documentation: https://app.aihub.qualcomm.com/docs/ for local development environment setup.

Evaluation Details

The evaluation data consist of the 64 classes from the 80 classes of COCO image detection. Each class has several images under different lighting conditions. Some classes have images generated with (GenAI Tool used for the generating synthetic images).
